Heavy Top Dynamic Problem

For this benchmark problem, we use the heavy top problem to evaluate the convergence rate of the Kynema Lie-group generalized-alpha time integrator. The problem is a rotating body fixed to the ground by a spherical joint and is described in detail in [@Bruls-etal:2012].

Our benchmark solution is a Kynema simulation with a time step size of \(10^{-5}\) seconds and we examine the vertical displacement at the end of two seconds.

Brüls, O., A. Cardona, and M. Arnold. 2012. “Lie Group Generalized-\(\alpha\) Time Integration Fo Constrained Flexible Multibody Systems.” Mechanism and Machine Theory 48: 121–137.
